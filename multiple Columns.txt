The Department was started with the sanctioned intake of 40 in the year 1997. The annual sanctioned intake was increased to 60 in the academic year 1998-99, to 90 in the academic year 1999-2000 and further increased to 120 in the academic year 2004-05. The Department also started its P.G. programme M.E. (Computer Science and Engineering) in the year 2004 with the sanctioned intake of 18. The Department is also a recognized Research Centre under Anna University since 2006. There are 2 Research Supervisors and 19 Research Scholars currently pursuing their research in the Department. The U.G. programme of the Department is accredited first in the year 2006 for 3 years and re-accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in the year 2013 for 2 years. The Department is currently in the process of renewal of its accreditation status.

The Department aims at providing value based training and skills needed for the students in the software industry. The Department has well equipped state-of-the-art laboratory facilities to carryout various real-time projects, research and consultancy activities in recent technologies. A dedicated team of 26 faculty members contribute towards holistic development of students.
